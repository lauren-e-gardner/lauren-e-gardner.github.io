"REALITY" ANIMATION


Class: Digital Animation [VIS 220]
Year: Fall 2022

Final Submitted Version

Time Frame: ~8 weeks
Assignment: Create a 1-3 minute animation with both audio and visual content.

For this digital animation assignment, I was inspired by the isolation that virtual reality technology creates when people use it to escape the real world.



My main character and setting designs emphasize the stark difference between the character's virtual world and reality. In the virtual world, she is calm and well rested wandering through a serene forest landscape, when, in reality, she has bags under her eyes from lack of sleep living in a loud, chaotic city.
