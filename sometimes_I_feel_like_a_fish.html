There Are Days Where I Feel Like a Fish

In the fall of 2023, I sought out to describe an indescribable feeling quite familiar to me. Weird, gross, and floppy, I often feel out of place in situations - like a fish out of water. This comic is not only a visual description of those feelings but also a story about accepting and embracing that feeling.
