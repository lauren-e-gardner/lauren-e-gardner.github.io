There Are Days Where I Feel Like a Fish



THERE ARE DAYS WHERE I FEEL LIKE A FISH


Class: How To Write a Graphic Novel [ATL 496]
Year: Fall 2023

Final Submitted Version

Assignment: Write, design, and publish a comic book page or full-length story.

I was inspired by the feeling of fishiness, which is a feeling quite familiar to me; a feeling of awkwardness, undisirability, and grossness brought on by situations encountered in my everyday life.



My comic depicts the story of two university students, who seem to have nothing in common, find their simularities through their fishiness. They both feel like a fish out of water in the normal world, and relate to each other based on this.



I chose to draw the comic in a clean cartoonish style with limited colors -- red and blue. I used these colors sparingly, to provide emphasis on the fishy moments in the commic. Otherwise, the comic is in black, white, and half-tones.



I decided to make my own font using my handwritting to keep an almost journalistic quality to the story while also making the creation process easier.
