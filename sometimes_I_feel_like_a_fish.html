There Are Days Where I Feel Like a Fish




THERE ARE DAYS WHERE I FEEL LIKE A FISH


Class: How To Write a Graphic Novel [ATL 496]
Year: Fall 2023

Final Submitted Version [Click Here]

Time Frame: ~12 weeks
Assignment: Write, design, and publish a comic book page or full-length story.





Sometimes we all feel like a fish out of water. My graphic novel depicts the story of two university students who share this fishy feeling.



I drew the novel in a clean, cartoonish style with black, white, and half-tones, limiting color to red and blue when emphasizing fishy moments.



Using my own handwriting as reference, I created this custom font to help give the novel a personal journal feel. I created the font using a website online and then uploading the font file to Procreate.
